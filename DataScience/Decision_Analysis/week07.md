### DSS Components

**1. Database Management Subsystem (DBMS)**

- a collection of programs for managing data and simultaneously it supports different types of users to create, manage, retrieve, update and store information.

**2. Model-based Management Subsystem (MBMS)**

- a component of a DSS that stores and accesses models that decision makers use to make decisions

**3. Knowledge-based (Management) Subsystem (KBS)**

- a form of artificial intelligence (AI) that aims to capture the knowledge of human experts to support decision-making.

**4. User Interface**

- a visual part of a system/application that
determines how a user interacts with it and how the
information is displayed on the screen.
